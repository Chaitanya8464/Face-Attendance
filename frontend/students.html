Registration Process
  1. Click "Register New Student"
  2. Enter student name and roll number
  3. System generates unique UID automatically
  4. Capture 5-10 face images from different angles
  5. Train the model to encode faces
Important Notes
  • Only admins can register students
  • Each student gets a unique UID
  • Students can only mark attendance after training
  • Capture faces in good lighting
  • Retrain after adding new students
